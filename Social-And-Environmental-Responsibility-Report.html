Our Annual Accounting to You

Our Annual
Accounting to You

2019 was a great year—from becoming a Certified B Corporation to starting our Community Grant Fund and initiating the certification process with Fair For Life, we have a lot to celebrate. And we know we couldn’t have done any of it without your support. Thank you!

For 24 years, we have been passionate about serving our customers while honoring the timeless wisdom of Ayurveda. And as we grow, so does our ability to raise the bar in our commitment to our people and our planet. This is our annual accounting to you, sharing our accomplishments and our challenges along the journey. Join us in taking a moment to reflect on 2019. Read the full report.
